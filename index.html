Apertus is an advanced large language model (LLM) developed by the Swiss AI Initiative, trained in a compliant way on vast amounts of multilingual data. It is designed as an experimental platform, offering broad insights, answers, and creative assistance on a wide range of topics. The Public AI Network is a global organization focused on building communities around models like Apertus, dedicated to ensuring transparency, openness, safety, and ethical use. The Public Inference Utility is a free and accessible platform, aiming to democratize AI for public benefit. Since the launch on September 2, 2025, Apertus has been available on this infrastructure, thanks to donated compute credits and volunteer efforts.

We are now starting an open local cooperative, the Swiss Public Inference Utility, to support and sustain this mission in Switzerland for years to come.

Global Access to Knowledge

Large language technologies, when developed as public service tools, can revolutionize access to information. Public AI is a movement that emphasizes sustainability and social impact, aiming to reduce reliance on proprietary models. It prioritizes sovereignty and neutrality to ensure unbiased services where users understand data handling and governance transparently. We run benchmarks, develop literature, consult civil society organizations and governments, and recommend open and sovereign tools.

Building more Inclusive AI

To build social impact, we need to access the complete lifecycle of AI development: starting with diverse datasets (e.g., Common Crawl, Wikipedia, OpenSubtitles) and open-source APIs (e.g., OpenRouter, Hugging Face). This allows us to create a comprehensive foundation that supports diversity and accessibility of the topic. By doing so, we aim to democratize AI by addressing gaps for underserved communities, offering free or inexpensive local support for Apertus users and service providers.

Join the Movement

Implementing a large language model as a public service tool can lead to significant societal impacts. It can become a trusted source of information, provide clear explanations, and ensure users can interact with AI safely and informedly. This approach seeks to shift the development landscape towards more inclusivity, responsibility, and community engagement.

Our focus today is on establishing:
  • A sustainable and transparent cooperative
  • Open source community governance
  • Local relevance and social impact
