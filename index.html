Empowering Global Access to Knowledge

Large language technologies, when developed as public service tools, can revolutionize access to information. Public AI is a movement that emphasizes sustainability and social impact, aiming to reduce reliance on proprietary models. It prioritizes sovereignty and neutrality to ensure unbiased services where users understand data handling and governance transparently.

Transforming Access to Knowledge and Building Inclusive AI

To build such a tool, we start with diverse datasets (e.g., Common Crawl, Wikipedia, OpenSubtitles) and open-source APIs (e.g., OpenRouter, Hugging Face). This allows us to create a comprehensive, multilingual foundation that supports global accessibility. By doing so, we aim to democratize access to knowledge, bridging gaps for underserved communities and offering personalized support in multiple languages.

Join the Movement for Inclusive and Responsible AI

Implementing a large language model as a public service tool can lead to significant societal impacts. It can become a trusted source of information, provide clear explanations, and ensure users can interact with AI safely and informedly. This approach seeks to shift the AI development landscape towards more inclusivity, responsibility, and community engagement.

In summary:

  • Multilingual support (60+ languages, including Swiss national languages)
  • Sustainable and transparent development process
  • Open-source and community-driven
